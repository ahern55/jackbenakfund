ABOUT

Jack Benak's Legacy

Jack was an incredible person and leader who left a lasting impression on people he met in all aspects of his life.

In school, Jack was a leader of Paint Crew, always happy to support his fellow students in their athletic endeavors. He was friendly with all groups at the high school, respectful to everyone.

On the pitch, Jack was a humble leader. A four-year starter and a senior captain, Jack was always one of the first to practice and one of the last to leave. He led through the example of his intense work ethic.

In the community, Jack was recognized by many for his vibrant smile and friendly personality. He also worked as one of the friendly faces at Handelâ€™s Ice Cream.

Scholastics

Jack graduated from Twinsburg High School in 2019 as a member of Economics Club and a Suburban League scholar athlete. He had begun studies at Wittenberg University in Graphic Design and Marketing.

His passion for graphic design also inspired him to start a clothing line, Blank Co.

Athletics

A four-year starter and senior captain of the Twinsburg Varsity Soccer Team, Jack left a huge impact on the program. Although named a captain for only his senior year, other players always looked to Jack for leadership. His dedication and hard work also led him to a starting role on the Wittenburg Varsity Soccer Team his freshman year.

Aspirations

While Jack was humble, he was also an incredibly driven individual. Jack often inspired friends and classmates with motivational text messages. Jack had several goals for life after college-- one of the most important of his goals was to become a graphic designer living in New York City. Additionally, Jack aspired to travel the world.
